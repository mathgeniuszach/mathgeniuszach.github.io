This is a neat little bloat free place to play games with your friends.

Click the button on the left panel to copy a join link. Send it to your friends and they can join your lobby!

You are the host of this lobby. This means you pick the games being played, but if you leave, the lobby will be disbanded and everyone will be disconnected.
